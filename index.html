Escape Room

In the land of Erathia, to unlock the ancient portal, one must decipher a 4-rune combination. Heed your inner oracle and remain vigilant in your surroundings. The spirits whisper the first rune to you: it is the number 5.

To proceed on your quest, return to the magical scrying screen and adjust its contrast as it was before. For the next rune, invoke the spell to put colors into force.

Remember! Once you close this scroll, it will be gone forever.
